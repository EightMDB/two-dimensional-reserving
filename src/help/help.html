Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported for data upload?

Currently, the application supports CSV files. Ensure your data includes columns for incurred date, paid date, paid amount, and category/LOB.

Which methodology should I use?

The choice depends on your data and requirements. Chain Ladder is best for stable patterns, while Bornhuetter-Ferguson works well when you have credible a priori loss ratios. See the Methodology Guide for detailed comparisons.

How do I export my results?

You can export results from the Analysis page or Report Builder. Supported formats include PDF, Excel, and CSV.
